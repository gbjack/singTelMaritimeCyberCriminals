Dear Customer,

According to former FBI Director Robert Mueller,
"there are only two types of companies, those that have been hacked, and those that will be."1

As cyber threats escalate and become more sophisticated, enterprises need to move beyond protecting the network and endpoints to adopt a proactive approach to cyber defense, one that incorporates cyber preparedness and threat intelligence.

Join us at Singtel TechTalks: Inside the Mind of a Cyber Criminal as cyber threat intelligence expert Brian Hussey examines the motivations and sophisticated strategies used by today’s cybercriminals.

At this event, we will:
• Explore case studies drawn from Trustwave SpiderLabs investigations, focusing on a recent attack that netted cybercriminals over $100M USD.
 
• Discuss how we must evolve beyond the old perimeter-focused security paradigm to engage all enterprise endpoints.
 
• Understand how to utilise threat intelligence to fuel security operations, and employ cyber methodologies such as threat hunting, incident readiness & response and deep-dive forensics.
